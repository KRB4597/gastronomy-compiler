Classic Caesar salad with housemade dressing.

The dressing is built by mashing six anchovies with two cloves of garlic into
a paste, then whisked with egg yolk, Dijon mustard, fresh lemon juice, and
Worcestershire sauce. Extra-virgin olive oil is drizzled in slowly to emulsify.
Finished with finely grated Parmigiano-Reggiano and black pepper.

Romaine lettuce hearts are torn and tossed with the dressing until every leaf
is coated. Topped with large rustic croutons made from day-old sourdough bread
toasted in olive oil with garlic, and a generous shaving of Parmigiano-Reggiano.

A final grind of coarse black pepper and a few drops of lemon juice to finish.
